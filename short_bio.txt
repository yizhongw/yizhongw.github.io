He has also been a student researcher at the Allen Institute for Artificial Intelligence (AI2) for the past 2 years, co-leading the post-training efforts in building fully open language models (OLMo). His research focuses on the fundamental data challenges in AI development and algorithms centered around data, particularly for building more general-purpose models.
