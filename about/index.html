Short Bio

Hey, there! Thanks for stopping by.

I am currently a first-year master student in the Computer Science Department at Peking University. I'm a member of the Institute of Computational Linguistics and work under the supervision of Prof. Li Sujian. Prior to that, I received B.Eng. in Computer Science from the IEEE Pilot Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, where I spent the best part of my time and found my life goals.

I've been steadily working on and have great interest in Natural Language Proceesing and Machine Learning, with current focus on Deep Learning. My current research areas include: discourse parsing, text representation and machine reading comprehension. I also develop some funny applications and websites during my spare time.
