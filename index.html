Short Bio

I am a seond-year PhD student at the University of Washington, where I am very fortunate to be co-advised by Hannaneh Hajishirzi and Noah Smith. Prior to UW, I completed my Master's degree at Peking University and Bachelor's degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research aims to push forward artificial intelligence through the language perspective. In particular, I am interested in how to make machines understand natural language, acquire knowledge from the textual world, and conduct systematic reasoning in complex scenarios. Currently, I evaluate my research goal mainly by various types of question answering tasks.

My name is written as 王义中 in Chinese characters.
